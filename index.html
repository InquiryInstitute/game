World Cup

We’re presenting The Game during World Cup. The beautiful game meets the life of the mind — when the world watches football, we invite you to watch philosophy play.

Tradition — The idea of a “game” that weaves together philosophy, culture, and play has a long history. Hermann Hesse’s novel Das Glasperlenspiel (The Glass Bead Game) imagined an intellectual game uniting music, mathematics, and the life of the mind. We don’t adapt that work; we share its spirit: inquiry as play, thought as spectacle. The Inquiry Cup is our pitch for that tradition.
